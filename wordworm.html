Start

For this project the task given to us was to make a new version of the hangman game, but with a twist. We were asked to make this kid friendly.

Prototype

The prototype you see here is an interactive prototype made in Figma. I called the my game WordWorm. The objective is to get the wordworm to eat through the whole apple. If you make more than 3 mistakes the worm will be stuck in the apple.
